Motivation

If you're in the labeling business, one key aspect of maintaining a competative edge is a thorough understanding of current marketing trends.

There are many questions you might ask:

What regional differences exist?
Are there any annual trends in color, phrasing, artwork?
What differences exist among labels on beer, wine, and spirits?

First steps

Gathering and interpretting data

Web scraping

The process begins with collecting data from the web. Scrapers pull down images and associated meta data.

Image analysis

Labels are scrutinized with machine learning algorithms, and only the results are saved to reduce space.

Storing it all

To effeciently store of all of this information a database is necessary. In this case postgres helps out.

Analysis

Finally, sythensizing data into comprehensive visualizations reveals key trends and answers to our questions.
